How to use this app
1
Dashboard — FDP Calculator
Enter your reporting time and select the number of landings. The app instantly shows your max FDP, max flight time, and the exact time your FDP expires. Night duty and WOCL are detected automatically based on your times.
2
Paste Roster — getting your text
Option A — Google Lens: Point your phone camera at your eCrew screen → tap Google Lens → it reads the text → tap "Copy all text".

Option B — Long press: In eCrew, long press on the roster text → Select All → Copy.

Paste into the Roster page. The app finds every duty with Reporting + Debriefing times, skips OFG/PL/off days, and checks all FDTL limits.
3
Log flights manually
Use the Log page to add duties one by one. Enter report and debrief times — FDP is calculated automatically. Night duty is auto-detected. All duties feed your cumulative limits on the Dashboard.
4
Install as an app on your phone
Open myindigofdtl.netlify.app in Chrome on your Android → tap the ⋮ menu → "Add to Home screen" → Install. Works offline for all features.
FDTL rules reference — IndiGo OMA Ch.2 TR13/2026
§2.6.12-pilot FDP limits (day duty)
6 ldg → max FT 8h / max FDP 11:00h
5 ldg → max FT 8h / max FDP 11:30h
4 ldg → max FT 8h / max FDP 12:00h
3 ldg → max FT 8h / max FDP 12:30h
2 ldg → max FT 9h / max FDP 13:00h
1 ldg → max FT 10h / max FDP 13:00h
§2.6.1.4Night duty (FDP encroaches 0000–0600 local)
Max FT 8h · Max FDP 10:00h · Max 2 landings
§2.6.2Duty period shall not exceed FDP + 1 hour
§2.4.1.2Post-flight duty: 30 min added after last engine off
§2.4.1.3Local night = 0000–0800 hrs local time
§2.3.25WOCL = 0200–0600 local (Window of Circadian Low). FDP starting in WOCL reduced by up to 2h. FDP ending in / encompassing WOCL reduced by 50%.
§2.7.1Augmented crew FDP (bunk):
3-crew → max FT 12h / FDP 16h / 2 ldg
4-crew → max FT 14h / FDP 18h / 1 ldg
3-crew iso seat → max FDP 15h
3-crew basic seat → max FDP 14h
§2.8Cumulative flight time limits:
7 days → 35h FT / 60h DP
14 days → 65h FT / 100h DP
28 days → 100h FT / 190h DP
90 days → 300h FT / 600h DP
365 days → 1000h FT / 1800h DP
§2.10.1Minimum rest before flight: Must be the GREATER of: (a) preceding FDP, OR (b) 12h domestic / 18h for 3–7 TZ crossings / 36h for >7 TZ crossings
§2.11Standby rules:
Max standby: 12h (home/hotel) · 8h at airport (§2.11.2.1)
Airport standby: 100% counts as cumulative duty. If culminates to flight → also counts as FDP (§2.11.2.2)
Home/hotel → flight, within 6h: no contribution to FDP or duty (§2.11.2.3a)
Home/hotel → flight, ≥6h: 50% of standby counts as FDP (§2.11.2.3b)
Home/hotel, no flight: 25% counts as cumulative duty (§2.11.2.4)
Rest after standby (no flight): min 12h (§2.10.3.1)
§2.10.6Weekly rest: Min 48 continuous hours including 2 local nights, within every 168-hour period
§2.13.1Consecutive night ops: Max 2 consecutive night duties (0000–0600) in any 168-hour period
§2.16.1Unforeseen extensions: FT +1.5h max, FDP +3h max. Cumulative limit: 4h FT and 8h FDP in any 28 days
